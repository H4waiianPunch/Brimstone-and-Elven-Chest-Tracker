From the Vibe Coder that brought you the Aerial Fishing Pearl Luck Tracker and Unsired Tracker plugins...

I present to you, the Brimstone and Elven Crystal Chest Tracker!

Here's what the overlays looks like in game! <br>

With this plugin, you will be able to see the following things:
-   The total number of chests opened for both the Brimstone chest and the Elven Crystal Chest
-   The last chest number opened that you received a clog or duplicate piece of their respective armours.
-   Longest Dry Streak (The most number of chests between 2 clogs or dupes)
-   Most Spooned (The fewest number of chests between 2 clogs or dupes)

You can reset the following stats:
- All Stats (Sets you back to 0 in everything, including current KC)
- Last Clog
- Last Dupe
- Longest Dry
- Most Spooned

These stats can be reset by Shift + Right-Clicking the overlay on your screen and selecting which option you want to reset! <br>
